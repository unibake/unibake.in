About Us

Unibake opened its first kiosk location in Benachity, Durgapur. It is known as bakery unique for its super delicious homemade cakes and mousse. We take pride in using natural ingredients in our products. Our treats are as deliciously healthy as they are beautifully embellished. Choose from our signature cakes, cupcakes, weekly and holiday specials or custom created desserts.
       Unibake offers a homestyle bakery experience in the Benachity,Durgapur area. Our vision is to provide goods and services to our customers with utmost professionalism amd with affordable products. We highly take pride in customizing our products to the needs of our customer.
